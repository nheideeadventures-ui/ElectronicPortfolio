About Me

My name is Nicole Heidee M. Kierulf, and I turned 14 on May 28. I consider myself a loving person because the main things God wants us to do is to love God and love all people. My hobbies are singing, acting, and writing, and playing sports. I like a lot of things, but when I think of my favorites, I think about God, my friends, and just being with the people I love. I dedicate my whole life and everything I do to God because He is the one who loves me unconditionally and will be there for me at all times. I want to live my life for Him and make sure that everything I do brings glory to His name.

Friends

My friends are the people that bring fun to my life and are my motivation to go to school. I believe that my friends right now are the people I've prayed for God to put in my life. I love them with my whole heart and I will forever treasure the relationship I have with each and every one of them. I hope our friendship lasts forever, because there's no other people I'd rather grow up with and be with for the rest of our lives.

Family

My whole family believes in God and worships Him with all our hearts. I am grateful to my parents for introducing me to God and for continuing to help me strengthen my relationship with Him. I love that I can say my family are good, God loving people and that everything we do should always be for Jesus and to glorify His name.
